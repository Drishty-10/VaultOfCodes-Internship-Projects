Chocolate Cake

Prep Time: 45 mins

Servings: 6

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix dry ingredients.
  3. Add eggs and milk.
  4. Pour into baking pan.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes.
